Gauntlet Legends Character Art Spec (Recolor-Safe)

Global rules (Grayscale-Only Pipeline, Recolor-Safe):
- Entire character rendered in grayscale ONLY with transparent background; clean alpha (no halos/matte), no drop shadows. Full body in frame (boots and full weapon visible).
- Four band families must be used, each strictly separated for shader mapping, avoiding absolute extremes (do not use pure #000/#FFF):
  1) Hair band (near-black, middle of the dark range): luma 24–72 only, flat-to-soft clustered fills, low saturation (neutral). Preserve form via value steps; no colored highlights.
  2) Skin band (near-white, middle-high range): luma 216–244 only for highlight planes (midtones/shadows must be darker than 216 and NOT in trim greys). Keep highlight flats crisply bounded; no color.
  3) Armour/Weapons band (mid greys): luma 96–200 for all base materials (metal, leather, cloth, wood) EXCLUDING trim and reserved hair/skin bands. Use painterly shading within this luma window.
  4) Trim bands (exact discrete greys for recolor): use ONLY these exact flat values—#202020 (32), #404040 (64), #606060 (96), #808080 (128). No gradients/dithering; crisp edges. Apply to: armor edge inlays, emblem outlines, belt piping, boot edges, bracer seams, bow limb edge accents, quiver/sash piping, shield rim inlays, staff grip rings.
- Prohibitions: Do NOT use any color; do NOT use pure #000000/#FFFFFF; do NOT reuse trim greys for any non-trim area; do NOT leak hair/skin bands into armor/weapons.
- Hair: only uses luma 24–72 (neutral), clustered values, clean silhouette suitable for tinting.
- Skin: only uses luma 216–244 on brightest planes; midtones should fall into the 140–210 range to keep painterly shading and avoid remapping.
- Anti-aliasing allowed against transparency but keep interior pixels strictly within respective bands.

Per character prompts (Greyscale-only):

Trim: apply ONLY #202020/#404040/#606060/#808080 to armor edge inlays, emblem outlines, belt/boot piping. Hair strictly 24–72 band. Skin highlights strictly 216–244 band. Transparent background, crisp silhouette.

Wizard:

Technical encoding checklist:
- Hair: luma strictly 24–72; Skin highlights: 216–244; Armour/Weapons base: 96–200; Trim: exactly {32,64,96,128}. No pixels may use 0–16 or 245–255 except anti-aliased transparent fringe; avoid trim greys outside trim. No halos; clean alpha.
